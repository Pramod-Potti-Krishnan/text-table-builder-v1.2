Cloud Deployment

Flexible OpEx model; scale resources as needed, pay only for usage.

Instantly scale resources up or down to meet fluctuating demands efficiently.

Provider handles infrastructure maintenance, updates, and patching entirely.

Access data and applications securely from any location with internet.

Benefit from robust provider security measures and compliance certifications.

Deploy new services and applications rapidly, accelerating time-to-market.

Leverage cutting-edge technologies and services without major investments.

On-Premise Deployment

Significant upfront capital expenditure for hardware, software, and setup.

Scaling requires manual hardware upgrades, leading to slower expansion.

Internal IT team manages all infrastructure, maintenance, and updates.

Access typically restricted to internal network or secure VPN connections.

Complete control over security measures and data privacy within your premises.

Longer deployment cycles due to procurement, installation, and configuration.

Innovation relies on internal R&D and capital investment in new technologies.

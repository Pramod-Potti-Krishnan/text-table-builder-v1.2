Public Cloud Model

Leverage massive scalability and elasticity for fluctuating demand spikes.

Reduce upfront capital expenditure; pay-as-you-go operational costs.

Accelerate time-to-market for new applications and services quickly.

Access cutting-edge technologies and global infrastructure resources.

Minimize infrastructure maintenance and operational overhead for teams.

Enable rapid prototyping and testing without significant investment.

Benefit from robust security and compliance certifications from providers.

Private Cloud Model

Maintain complete control over data, security, and regulatory compliance.

Ensure dedicated resources for sensitive workloads and critical applications.

Optimize performance for specific enterprise applications with custom hardware.

Achieve predictable costs for stable, long-term infrastructure requirements.

Implement tailored security policies and network isolation for data.

Support legacy systems and existing on-premises investments effectively.

Facilitate internal innovation with a fully managed, owned environment.

Hybrid Cloud Model

Combine public agility with private control for optimal workload placement.

Migrate workloads seamlessly between environments based on business needs.

Optimize costs by leveraging public cloud for variable loads, private for stable.

Enhance disaster recovery and business continuity strategies effectively.

Address data residency and compliance requirements with flexible options.

Extend existing data centers with public cloud resources for burst capacity.

Maximize flexibility and adaptability for evolving strategic business goals.

Ideate & Discover

Build initial prototypes or minimum viable products (MVPs).

Conduct rigorous testing to validate functionality and design.

Gather user feedback to identify areas for improvement iteratively.

Refine the solution based on test results and user insights.

Iterate quickly to optimize the product for market readiness.

Scale & Deliver

Develop a comprehensive go-to-market strategy for success.

Execute the launch plan with precision and strategic marketing.

Monitor market reception and performance post-launch closely.

Provide ongoing support and gather continuous customer feedback.

Plan for future enhancements and scaling based on market data.

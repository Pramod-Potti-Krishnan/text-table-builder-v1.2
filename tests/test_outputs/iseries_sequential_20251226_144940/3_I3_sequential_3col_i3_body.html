1

Sprint Planning

Define sprint goals aligning with strategic objectives.

Prioritize backlog items based on business value and impact.

Allocate resources and assign tasks for efficient execution.

Establish clear success criteria for deliverables.

2

Execute & Develop

Develop features and solutions according to sprint plan.

Collaborate daily to address impediments and ensure progress.

Conduct continuous testing to maintain high quality standards.

Adapt to new information, adjusting priorities as needed.

3

Evaluate & Adapt

Present completed work to stakeholders for feedback.

Assess sprint outcomes against predefined success metrics.

Identify areas for process improvement and team growth.

Incorporated learnings into the next planning cycle.

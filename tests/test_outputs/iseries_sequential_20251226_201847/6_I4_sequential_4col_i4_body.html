1

Pre-Launch Strategy

Define target audience and market needs.

Develop comprehensive marketing plan.

Prepare internal teams and resources.

Finalize product features and readiness.

Set clear launch objectives and metrics.

2

Soft Launch Phase

Release product to a limited audience.

Gather initial user feedback diligently.

Identify and resolve any critical issues.

Test systems and infrastructure scalability.

Iterate on product based on insights gained.

3

Full Launch Execution

Officially release product to broad market.

Execute full-scale marketing campaigns.

Ensure consistent customer support readiness.

Monitor performance and sales closely.

Scale operations to meet demand effectively.

4

Post-Launch Growth

Continuously monitor product performance.

Analyze user data for future improvements.

Plan for updates, new features, and iterations.

Maintain strong customer engagement efforts.

Evaluate launch success against objectives.

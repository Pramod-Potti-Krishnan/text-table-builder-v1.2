1

Plan the Sprint

Define sprint goals and scope based on product backlog.

Select high-priority items from the refined backlog.

Break down tasks and estimate effort for each item.

Team commits to delivering specific work by sprint end.

Establish clear acceptance criteria for all features.

2

Build the Product

Develop features daily, focusing on collaboration.

Conduct daily stand-ups to track progress and sync.

Address impediments quickly to maintain velocity.

Implement rigorous testing throughout the development.

Ensure code quality and adherence to definitions of done.

3

Review & Adapt

Present completed work to stakeholders for feedback.

Gather insights on delivered features and functionality.

Conduct a sprint retrospective to identify improvements.

Implement process adjustments for future sprints.

Refine product backlog based on new learnings and feedback.

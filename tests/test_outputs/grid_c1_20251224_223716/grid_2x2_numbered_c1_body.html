1

Project Scoping & Design

  • Define comprehensive project objectives, scope, and key success metrics with stakeholders.
  • Conduct detailed requirements gathering and business process analysis to align with goals.
  • Develop the technical architecture and solution design, ensuring scalability and security.
  • Establish project timelines, resource allocation, and risk mitigation strategies proactively.
2

Build & Customization

  • Execute core system development and module configuration based on approved design specifications.
  • Integrate new components with existing infrastructure, ensuring seamless data flow and compatibility.
  • Develop necessary custom features and reports to meet specific organizational requirements.
  • Implement robust data migration strategies and prepare for data transfer from legacy systems.
3

Quality Assurance & UAT

  • Perform rigorous functional, integration, and performance testing to identify and resolve issues.
  • Conduct user acceptance testing (UAT) with key stakeholders to validate system functionality.
  • Document all test cases, results, and defect resolutions, ensuring full traceability and quality.
  • Provide comprehensive training sessions for end-users to ensure smooth adoption and proficiency.
4

Go-Live & Post-Launch

  • Execute the planned system deployment, including final data migration and environment setup.
  • Monitor system performance and stability immediately post-launch to address any critical issues.
  • Establish a continuous improvement process for ongoing system enhancements and optimization.
  • Conduct post-implementation reviews to evaluate success metrics and capture lessons learned.

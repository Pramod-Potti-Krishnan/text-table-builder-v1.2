1

Pre-boarding Essentials

  • Complete all necessary HR paperwork and background checks prior to the employee's start date.
  • Ensure IT equipment, system access, and workstation are fully prepared and operational on day one.
  • Prepare a personalized welcome kit including company swag and essential first-day information.
2

Initial Welcome & Setup

  • Conduct a warm personal welcome, introductions to key team members, and a comprehensive office tour.
  • Review the first-day schedule, immediate priorities, and provide an overview of company culture.
  • Assist with initial login procedures and ensure seamless access to all necessary internal platforms.
3

Role & Team Integration

  • Facilitate introductory meetings with direct team members and cross-functional colleagues to build rapport.
  • Clearly define job responsibilities, performance expectations, and key objectives for the first 90 days.
  • Assign a buddy or mentor to guide the new hire through early challenges and company nuances effectively.
4

Essential Training

  • Provide access to mandatory compliance training modules and company-specific software tutorials promptly.
  • Schedule product or service knowledge sessions to rapidly build foundational understanding of offerings.
  • Offer opportunities for skill enhancement workshops relevant to their role and future career growth.
5

Performance & Feedback

  • Establish clear, measurable performance goals and milestones within the first few weeks of employment.
  • Conduct regular one-on-one check-ins to discuss progress, address concerns, and provide constructive feedback.
  • Schedule a formal 30, 60, or 90-day review to assess integration and initial performance comprehensively.
6

Ongoing Engagement

  • Discuss long-term career development paths and available internal growth opportunities within the organization.
  • Encourage participation in company events, employee resource groups, and team-building activities actively.
  • Implement a continuous learning strategy, offering access to advanced courses and professional certifications.

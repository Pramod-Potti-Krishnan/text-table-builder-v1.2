1

Empathize & Define

Understand users deeply through research and observation.

Engage stakeholders to uncover their true needs and pain points.

Synthesize collected insights to identify core user problems.

Clearly articulate the problem statement from the user's perspective.

2

Ideate & Prototype

Brainstorm diverse solutions without initial judgment or constraints.

Encourage creative thinking to generate a wide range of ideas.

Select promising concepts to develop further into tangible forms.

Build quick, low-fidelity prototypes to visualize potential solutions.

3

Test & Iterate

Present prototypes to actual users for valuable, honest feedback.

Observe user interactions and gather insights on prototype usability.

Analyze feedback to identify areas for improvement and refinement.

Modify and enhance solutions based on user testing observations.

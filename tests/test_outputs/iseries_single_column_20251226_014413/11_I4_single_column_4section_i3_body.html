Idea Generation & Discovery

  • Identify emerging trends and market opportunities.
  • Conduct thorough competitive and customer needs analysis.
  • Assess technical feasibility and potential business impact.

Concept Development

  • Design initial solution architecture and user experience.
  • Build Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for core functionality.
  • Develop functional models for internal review and testing.

Market Validation

  • Conduct targeted user feedback sessions and pilot programs.
  • Gather quantitative and qualitative data on performance.
  • Assess product-market fit and identify areas for improvement.

Commercialization & Launch

  • Develop comprehensive go-to-market strategy and plan.
  • Allocate resources for production, marketing, and sales.
  • Execute product launch and monitor initial market reception.

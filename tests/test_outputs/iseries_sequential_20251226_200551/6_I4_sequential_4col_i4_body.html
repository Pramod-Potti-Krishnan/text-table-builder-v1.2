1

Pre-Launch Prep

Define target audience and market.

Develop core messaging and positioning.

Plan marketing and communication strategy.

Conduct internal training and readiness.

Set key performance indicators (KPIs).

2

Soft Launch Phase

Release to limited user group.

Gather initial feedback and data.

Identify and resolve critical issues.

Test product stability and scalability.

Refine user experience based on input.

3

Full Scale Launch

Execute comprehensive marketing campaign.

Announce product to general public.

Ensure robust customer support is ready.

Monitor sales and initial adoption rates.

Engage with media and industry influencers.

4

Post-Launch Phase

Collect ongoing user feedback and metrics.

Analyze performance against set KPIs.

Plan for product updates and enhancements.

Evaluate overall launch success and ROI.

Document lessons learned for future launches.

1

Empathize & Define

Understand user needs through observation and engagement.

Gather insights into behaviors, motivations, and pain points.

Synthesize research to identify core problems and opportunities.

Formulate a clear, human-centered problem statement.

Define the specific challenge to be addressed effectively.

2

Ideate & Prototype

Brainstorm diverse solutions for the defined problem statement.

Encourage wild ideas without immediate judgment or critique.

Develop tangible representations of selected concepts quickly.

Create low-fidelity prototypes to visualize potential solutions.

Focus on functionality over aesthetics in this initial stage.

3

Test & Iterate

Evaluate prototypes with target users to gather crucial feedback.

Observe user interactions and identify areas for improvement.

Synthesize findings to understand what works and what doesn't.

Refine solutions based on user insights in an iterative cycle.

Repeat testing and refinement until the solution meets needs.

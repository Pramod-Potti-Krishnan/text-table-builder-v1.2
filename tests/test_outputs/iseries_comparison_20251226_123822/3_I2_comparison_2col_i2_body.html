Cloud Solution Benefits

Reduced upfront capital expenditure; pay-as-you-go model.

Scale resources up or down quickly based on demand fluctuations.

Vendor manages infrastructure, reducing internal IT overhead.

Access data and applications from anywhere, enhancing flexibility.

Automatic updates and access to latest technologies.

Built-in disaster recovery and robust data backup capabilities.

Shared security model; vendor handles infrastructure security.

On-Premise Considerations

Significant upfront capital investment for hardware and setup.

Scaling requires manual hardware upgrades and planning cycles.

Full control over infrastructure, demanding internal IT resources.

Access often limited to corporate network or VPN connections.

Manual updates; slower adoption of new technologies.

Requires internal planning and investment for DR solutions.

Complete control over security, but full responsibility internally.

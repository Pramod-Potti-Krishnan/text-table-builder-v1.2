Public Cloud

Leverage shared resources for rapid deployment and immense scalability.

Optimize costs with a pay-as-you-go model for variable workloads.

Access global infrastructure, enhancing reach and disaster recovery.

Reduce operational overhead; provider manages underlying infrastructure.

Best for non-sensitive data and standard business applications.

Benefit from continuous innovation and updated service offerings.

Consider data residency and compliance requirements carefully.

Private Cloud

Maintain full control over data security and regulatory compliance.

Utilize dedicated resources for predictable performance and isolation.

Customize infrastructure to meet unique business requirements precisely.

Suitable for highly sensitive data and mission-critical applications.

Incur higher upfront costs and ongoing management responsibilities.

Deploy on-premises or with a dedicated third-party provider.

Ensure robust internal IT expertise for effective management.

Hybrid Cloud

Combine public and private clouds for optimal flexibility and agility.

Place sensitive workloads on private, less sensitive on public.

Enable seamless data and application portability between environments.

Optimize costs by leveraging public cloud for burst capacity needs.

Enhance business continuity and disaster recovery strategies.

Requires sophisticated management tools and integration expertise.

Define clear strategies for workload placement and data governance.

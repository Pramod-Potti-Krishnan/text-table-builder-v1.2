Traditional Manual Process

Time-consuming tasks requiring significant human effort and attention to detail.

Higher risk of human error leading to rework, inconsistencies, and compliance issues.

Limited scalability, struggling to handle increased volumes without proportional resource growth.

Incurs higher operational costs due to labor-intensive efforts and potential error correction.

Repetitive tasks can lead to lower employee morale and reduced job satisfaction over time.

Challenges in collecting and analyzing data for performance insights and strategic decision-making.

Slower process execution, impacting overall turnaround times and market responsiveness.

Proposed Automated System

Significantly reduces processing time, enabling faster task completion and higher throughput.

Minimizes human error, ensuring consistent, accurate results and improved compliance.

Easily scales to accommodate fluctuating workloads without requiring additional human resources.

Lowers long-term operational costs by optimizing resource allocation and reducing manual labor.

Frees up employees to focus on strategic, value-added activities, boosting engagement.

Provides robust data collection and analytics for real-time insights and informed decisions.

Enhances process agility and responsiveness, adapting quickly to changing business demands.

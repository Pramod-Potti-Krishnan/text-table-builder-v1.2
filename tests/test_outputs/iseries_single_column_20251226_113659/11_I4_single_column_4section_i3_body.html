1. Ideation & Research

  • Identify market needs and emerging trends for new opportunities.
  • Conduct thorough feasibility studies and competitive analysis.
  • Define core problem statements and potential solution hypotheses.

2. Concept & Prototype

  • Develop initial concepts and low-fidelity prototypes for ideas.
  • Build functional minimum viable products (MVPs) for testing.
  • Gather internal feedback on usability and technical viability.

3. Test & Validate

  • Conduct rigorous user testing and gather external market feedback.
  • Validate product-market fit and refine value proposition.
  • Analyze KPIs and metrics to validate concept viability.

4. Deploy & Scale

  • Develop comprehensive go-to-market strategies and launch plans.
  • Allocate resources for full-scale development and market entry.
  • Monitor post-launch performance and plan for continuous improvement.

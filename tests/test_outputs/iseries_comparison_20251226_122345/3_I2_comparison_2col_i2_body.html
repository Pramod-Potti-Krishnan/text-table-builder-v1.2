Cloud Solution Advantages

Easily scale resources up or down based on demand; high flexibility.

Shift from CAPEX to OPEX, reducing significant upfront investment.

Vendor manages infrastructure, reducing internal IT operational burden.

Access data and applications from anywhere, enhancing remote work.

Leverage cutting-edge technologies and services from providers.

Built-in redundancy and DR capabilities, improving business continuity.

Rapid deployment of new services and features, accelerating innovation.

On-Premise Solution Factors

Full control over hardware, software, and data; custom configurations.

Maintain absolute data sovereignty and meet strict compliance needs.

Optimized performance for specific workloads with dedicated resources.

Significant upfront capital expenditure for infrastructure and licenses.

Requires dedicated IT staff for hardware, software, and security upkeep.

Scaling requires manual hardware upgrades, leading to longer lead times.

Less reliance on external vendors; direct management of dependencies.

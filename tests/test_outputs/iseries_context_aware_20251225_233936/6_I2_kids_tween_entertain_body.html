Wonder & Ask!

Look around with curious eyes.

What do you notice that's new?

Ask 'Why?' and 'How does that work?'

Let your questions guide your journey.

Experiment & Test!

Design a fun way to test your idea.

Gather materials and set things up.

Try it out and see what happens next.

Record your observations carefully.

3

Discover & Learn!

Look closely at your experiment results.

What did you find out about your idea?

Understand why things happened that way.

Celebrate your new scientific knowledge.

4

Share Your Science!

Tell friends and family what you learned.

Show off your cool discoveries with pride.

Inspire others to start their own quest.

Science is more fun when you share it!

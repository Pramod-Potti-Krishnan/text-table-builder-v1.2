1

Pre-Launch Planning

Define target market and core value proposition.

Develop comprehensive marketing and sales strategy.

Ensure internal teams are trained and ready for support.

Conduct final product testing and legal reviews.

2

Controlled Release

Launch to a select, targeted segment of users.

Gather critical feedback on performance and usability.

Iterate product features based on early user insights.

Assess market reception and refine messaging strategy.

3

Market-Wide Rollout

Execute full-scale marketing campaigns and promotions.

Activate all sales channels and distribution networks.

Scale customer support and infrastructure effectively.

Monitor initial sales and user adoption metrics closely.

4

Sustain & Optimize

Continuously monitor product performance and user data.

Gather ongoing feedback for future enhancements and fixes.

Plan for product updates, new features, and expansions.

Analyze ROI and adjust long-term strategy as needed.

Cloud Solution Advantages

Reduced upfront CapEx; flexible operational expense model.

Achieve rapid scalability and elasticity based on demand.

Offload infrastructure maintenance to cloud provider.

Global accessibility to data and applications from anywhere.

Leverage cutting-edge services and rapid innovation cycles.

Enhanced disaster recovery and robust data backup options.

Accelerate deployment times for new applications and services.

On-Premise Deployment

Requires significant upfront capital investment (CapEx).

Scaling involves manual hardware procurement and setup effort.

Full responsibility for all infrastructure maintenance.

Retain complete control over data, security, and hardware.

Allows deep customization to meet specific business needs.

Potentially simpler to meet specific regulatory compliance.

Optimize performance with direct hardware and network control.

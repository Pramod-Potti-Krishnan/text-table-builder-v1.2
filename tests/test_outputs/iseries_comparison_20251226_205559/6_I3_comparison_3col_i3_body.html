Public Cloud Model

Leverage massive scalability & rapid provisioning on demand.

Optimize costs with pay-as-you-go pricing for variable needs.

Access advanced services; no infrastructure management required.

Benefit from vendor-managed security, updates, and maintenance.

Accelerate innovation with instant access to new technologies.

Expand global reach easily with distributed data centers.

Reduce capital expenditure; shift to operational expenses.

Private Cloud Benefits

Maintain full control over data, security, and infrastructure.

Ensure strict compliance with industry-specific regulations.

Utilize dedicated resources for predictable performance & isolation.

Customize environment to specific organizational requirements.

Enhance data residency and sovereignty for sensitive workloads.

Greater transparency in resource usage and cost allocation.

Ideal for mission-critical applications requiring high security.

Hybrid Cloud Strategy

Combine public agility with private control for optimal balance.

Place workloads strategically based on security & performance.

Enable seamless data portability between environments.

Optimize costs by bursting to public cloud for peak demands.

Enhance disaster recovery and business continuity capabilities.

Modernize legacy applications while maintaining existing systems.

Achieve maximum flexibility and adaptability for future needs.

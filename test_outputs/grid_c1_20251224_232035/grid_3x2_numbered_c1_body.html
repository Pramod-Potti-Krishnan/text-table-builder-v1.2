1

Pre-Arrival Setup

  • Complete all necessary administrative tasks and system access provisioning before the start date.
  • Prepare the new hire's workspace, equipment, and welcome kit for a seamless first impression.
2

First Day Welcome

  • Provide a warm introduction to the team and key colleagues, fostering an inclusive environment.
  • Review company culture, values, and essential policies to establish foundational understanding.
3

Orientation & Training

  • Deliver comprehensive training on critical tools, systems, and job-specific responsibilities.
  • Assign a dedicated buddy or mentor to guide the new hire through initial challenges and questions.
4

Role Integration

  • Clearly define performance expectations and initial projects to ensure active contribution quickly.
  • Facilitate regular check-ins with the manager to discuss progress and address any emerging concerns.
5

Performance & Feedback

  • Conduct a formal 30-60-90 day review to assess performance, provide feedback, and set future goals.
  • Encourage new hires to provide feedback on the onboarding process for continuous improvement.
6

Ongoing Development

  • Identify opportunities for continuous learning and professional growth within their role and career path.
  • Ensure integration into team activities and company events to strengthen belonging and engagement.

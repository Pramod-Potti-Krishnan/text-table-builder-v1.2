1. Orientation

  • Welcome new hires to the team.
  • Complete all necessary HR paperwork.
  • Introduce company culture and values.
  • Provide initial team introductions.

2. Training

  • Provide job-specific skill development.
  • Grant access to all required systems.
  • Familiarize with essential tools and software.
  • Review key company policies.

3. Mentorship

  • Assign a dedicated mentor to each new hire.
  • Facilitate regular check-in meetings.
  • Provide ongoing guidance and support.
  • Encourage team integration.

4. Evaluation

  • Conduct initial performance reviews.
  • Gather feedback from new employees.
  • Set clear short-term and long-term goals.
  • Track overall integration progress.

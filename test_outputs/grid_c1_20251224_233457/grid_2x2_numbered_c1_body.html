1

Strategic Planning

  • Define comprehensive project scope, objectives, and key performance indicators with stakeholders.
  • Allocate necessary resources, establish project timelines, and identify potential risks proactively.
  • Develop a detailed implementation plan, including technical specifications and data migration strategies.
2

System Build & Setup

  • Configure system modules and integrate with existing infrastructure according to design specifications.
  • Develop custom functionalities and reports to meet specific business requirements and user needs.
  • Ensure all data structures are properly defined and initial data sets are prepared for migration.
3

Quality Assurance

  • Execute rigorous unit, integration, and system tests to ensure functionality and performance standards.
  • Conduct user acceptance testing (UAT) with key stakeholders to validate alignment with business processes.
  • Address all identified defects and perform regression testing to confirm resolution without new issues.
4

Go-Live & Support

  • Execute the final deployment plan, transitioning the new system into the production environment carefully.
  • Provide post-implementation support and training to end-users for smooth adoption and operational efficiency.
  • Continuously monitor system performance and gather feedback for ongoing optimization and future enhancements.

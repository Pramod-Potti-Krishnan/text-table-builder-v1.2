1

Pre-Boarding & Welcome

  • Initiate pre-arrival communications, including welcome kits and essential HR documentation.
  • Ensure workstation setup, system access, and initial schedule are prepared before day one.
2

First Day Essentials

  • Conduct official HR orientation, covering company policies, benefits, and compliance requirements.
  • Facilitate initial team introductions and a comprehensive office tour for new employees.
3

Role & Team Integration

  • Clearly define job responsibilities, performance expectations, and key departmental objectives.
  • Assign a dedicated mentor or buddy to provide ongoing support and cultural guidance.
4

Training & Development

  • Provide access to necessary tools, software training, and role-specific skill development programs.
  • Schedule foundational learning modules to ensure understanding of core business processes.
5

Performance & Feedback

  • Establish initial performance goals and discuss metrics for success within the first 30-60 days.
  • Conduct regular check-ins and structured feedback sessions to support early progress and adjustment.
6

Ongoing Support & Engagement

  • Encourage participation in company events, employee resource groups, and professional networking.
  • Develop a long-term development plan, identifying growth opportunities and career progression.

Identifying Potential Threats

  • Systematically uncover all internal and external vulnerabilities and hazards to operations.
  • Categorize identified risks by type, source, and potential area of impact for clarity.

Assessing Severity and Likelihood

  • Evaluate the probability of each risk event occurring and its potential magnitude of disruption.
  • Quantify the financial, reputational, and operational consequences for informed decisions.

Developing Control Measures

  • Implement proactive preventive controls to reduce risk exposure and event occurrence.
  • Establish robust contingency plans and response protocols for unavoidable risk scenarios.

Continuous Oversight & Adaptation

  • Regularly monitor key risk indicators (KRIs) to detect emerging threats and changes.
  • Periodically re-evaluate risk profiles and update mitigation strategies for effectiveness.

Stakeholder Engagement & Reporting

  • Provide clear, concise risk summaries and status updates to executive leadership.
  • Foster transparent communication and accountability across all organizational levels.

Quick Wins

  • Identify initiatives delivering significant value with minimal resources.
  • Focus on immediate implementation to build momentum and demonstrate value.
  • Capitalize on these opportunities for rapid, visible organizational gains.

Major Value Drivers

  • Allocate necessary resources for projects with substantial strategic returns.
  • Develop comprehensive plans to manage scope, timeline, and deliverables.
  • Monitor progress closely to ensure sustained alignment with objectives.

Strategic Investments

  • Prioritize long-term projects critical for future growth and market.
  • Ensure executive sponsorship and substantial resource allocation.
  • Conduct comprehensive risk assessment and contingency planning.

Routine Tasks

  • Delegate or automate these tasks to free up higher-priority resources.
  • Complete quickly as time permits, without diverting significant attention.
  • Evaluate if these activities can be streamlined or eliminated entirely.

Re-evaluate & Optimize

  • Scrutinize projects for potential value increase or scope reduction.
  • Consider if current resource allocation justifies expected outcomes.
  • Seek efficiencies or alternative approaches to minimize effort expenditure.

Deprioritize & Avoid

  • Actively remove these projects from current workstreams to conserve resources.
  • Challenge necessity and strategic alignment of these resource drains.
  • Reallocate resources from these efforts to higher-priority initiatives.

Immediate Action Projects

  • Secure resources for immediate execution and rapid deployment.
  • Expect significant ROI and market advantage within next quarter.
  • Align with core strategic objectives; high stakeholder buy-in.

Strategic Optimization

  • Enhance existing processes to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
  • Implement phased approach, leveraging current team capabilities.
  • Monitor performance metrics closely for iterative improvements.

Operational Enhancements

  • Address minor pain points with minimal resource allocation.
  • Delegate to operational teams for quick, routine implementation.
  • Ensure these do not divert focus from higher priority initiatives.

Visionary Investments

  • Conduct thorough feasibility studies and detailed risk assessments.
  • Allocate dedicated R&D budget for complex technological hurdles.
  • Define clear milestones for long-term strategic value realization.

Future Exploration

  • Re-evaluate resource availability and potential external partnerships.
  • Periodically review market shifts that could increase project urgency.
  • Maintain in backlog; revisit when strategic context or resources change.

Re-evaluate or Discard

  • Postpone indefinitely due to low strategic alignment and high risk.
  • Reassess value proposition; consider alternative, simpler solutions.
  • Avoid expending resources on projects with limited business return.
